Eligibility Criteria

Check if you meet our program requirements

B.Com Program

  • Passed 10+2 or equivalent examination
  • Minimum 45% aggregate in qualifying examination
  • Commerce/Arts/Science stream accepted
  • Working professionals are encouraged to apply

BBA Program

  • Passed 10+2 or equivalent examination
  • Minimum 45% aggregate in qualifying examination
  • Any stream accepted
  • Basic mathematics knowledge required

BCA Program

  • Passed 10+2 or equivalent examination
  • Minimum 45% aggregate in qualifying examination
  • Mathematics at 10+2 level preferred
  • Basic computer knowledge required

Required Documents

Please keep the following documents ready for admission

  • 10th Standard Marks Card (Original + 2 Copies)
  • 12th Standard Marks Card (Original + 2 Copies)
  • Transfer Certificate from previous institution
  • Migration Certificate (if applicable)
  • Character Certificate from previous institution
  • Passport size photographs (6 copies)
  • Aadhar Card Copy
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Income Certificate (if applicable)
  • Employment Certificate (for working professionals)
