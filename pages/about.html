About MGMEC

Empowering Working Professionals Through Education Since 2022

Preamble

Mahatma Gandhi Memorial Evening College (MGMEC) founded in 2022, is a new venture of M.G.M. College Trust. It is sponsored by the Academy of General Education, Manipal and affiliated to the Mangalore University.

It is situated on the Udupi-Manipal Highway two kilometers away from the temple city, Udupi. Udupi city is well connected by rail and bus services. Mangalore airport is only 58 Kms away from Udupi. National Highways NH 66 and NH 169A pass through Udupi. Private as well as government buses connect Udupi to all parts of Karnataka. The nearest harbour/port to Udupi is Malpe, which is 5km away. The New Mangalore Port is 50km away from Udupi.

Temple City

Udupi City is popularly called as 'Temple City' is situated about 55km north of Mangalore and about 422 km west of capital Bangalore by road. It is the administrative headquarters of Udupi District, and one of the fastest-growing cities in Karnataka.

In the 13th century, Vaishnavite Saint Madhvacharya founded the Sri Krishna Temple. He set up eight mathas - Ashta Mathas in Udupi to propagate the Dvaita Vedanta Philosophy.

Cultural Highlights

  • Traditional Arts: Bhootakola, Aatikalenja, Karangolu
  • Folk Art: Yakshagana with Badagu and Tenku Tittus
  • Major Festivals: Makara Sankranti, Krishna Janmashtami, Navaratri
  • Famous for: Udupi Cuisine and Temple Culture

Educational Hub

Manipal is a small town popularly known as the 'Educational and Medical Hub'. A world-class Private Deemed University, Manipal Academy for Higher Education (MAHE) is functioning on this small town. It is a globally engaged institution with active partnership with more than 220 leading universities around the globe.

Key Institutions

  • Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE)
  • Kasturba Medical College
  • Manipal Institute of Technology (MIT)
  • Various International Partnerships

MGM Evening College

MGM Evening College is an offshoot of Mahatma Gandhi Memorial (MGM) College, founded in 1949 by a great visionary, educationist, and banker, Padmashree Dr. T.M.A. Pai. It has a rich legacy and glorious past.

This college is established mainly to cater to the needs of working professionals and students who aim to use their morning time by enhancing their skills or work part-time.

Vision

To impart the need-based, skill-integrated, cost-effective, quality, and holistic education to the working youth who wish to continue their education while earning.

Mission

To provide the youth with all possible opportunities of graduation and to enhance educational facility for those who work during the day for their livelihood and wish to continue higher education.
