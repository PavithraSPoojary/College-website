About MGM Evening College

Established in 2022 to provide quality education to working professionals

MGM Evening College is an offshoot of Mahatma Gandhi Memorial (MGM) College, founded in 1949 by a great visionary, educationist, and banker, Padmashree Dr. T.M.A. Pai. It has a rich legacy and glorious past. This college is established mainly to cater to the needs of working professionals and students who aim to use their morning time by enhancing their skills or work part-time.
